Project Intern · Centre for Artificial Intelligence & Robotics, DRDO

Hands-on work in modeling, validation, sensor calibration, and corrective algorithm development for robotic subsystems.

Overview

This internship emphasized structured debugging, subsystem bring-up, sensor stability, and fault-oriented engineering practices in a robotics context.

What I did

  • Modeled, tested, and validated robotic subsystems using ROS2 and Gazebo, applying structured debugging and system bring-up practices.
  • Performed calibration and fine-tuning of multiple onboard sensors, improving noise behavior, parameter stability, and measurement accuracy.
  • Analyzed system deviations and implemented corrective algorithms using root-cause methodology.

Why it matters

  • Demonstrates strong fundamentals in validation and reliability-driven engineering.
  • Shows practical experience connecting models, sensors, and system behavior.
  • Supports roles that value disciplined testing, debugging, and performance monitoring.
